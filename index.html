About Me

I'm an artist at heart! I love to create!

For as long as I can remember, I've always wanted to make things. As a child, I took my inspirations from anime and videogames, incorporating that aesthetic into my own and making my own art style. Since starting at Thinkul, I've already learned so much. Let me make you something sometime.
